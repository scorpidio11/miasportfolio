About

A designer whose path evolved from fine art and spatial design into digital systems, email design, front-end thinking, and AI-assisted workflow tools.

Digital Design Web Design Email Design Brand Identity Vibe Coding Design System

My design roots trace back to fine art — recognized early through a national competition, and deepened through art-focused education and a spatial design major.

During my MFA at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, experimental virtual environments shifted my focus toward digital. That pivot led me into digital design, full-stack coding and email design + system development.

Today, I work at the intersection of design and technology — building email design systems and AI-assisted workflows that prioritize consistency, production efficiency, and cohesive digital experiences.
